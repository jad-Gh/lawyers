About the Founder

Holding a Bachelor’s degree in law from the Lebanese University – French Section , a Master’s degree in National and International Business Law from the Lebanese University in collaboration with the University of Aix-Marseille, a Master’s degree in Business Law from Paris Est and a Ph.D. in Private Law, specialty Business Law from Paris Est and Lebanese University (EDDSPAE).

Tamara Baydoun has more than 15 years of experience as a lawyer and in house legal counsel for a group of companies based in UAE and having subsidiaries in more than 25 countries in Europe, USA and Asia. She created more than 20 companies and made sure they applied laws and regulations and was responsible of putting in place compliance and GDPR manuals in a French company, established a tracker for regulatory changes required based on industry and stakeholder feedback, working as lawyer and external DPO of French companies and certified GDPR trainer since June 2023.
